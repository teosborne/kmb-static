Our Story

  • September 24, 2012

    The Interview

    Youn applied for a job at the Wharton Small Business Development Center, where she was interviewed by Tom (and Leslie Mitts, who will also be in the ceremony). She passed!

  • October 27, 2012

    First Dates

    Youn attended Tom’s Halloween pre-game and the Wharton Halloween party. Coincidentally, Youn dressed as Marilyn Monroe (American) and Tom went as the elevator guy from Gangnam Style (Korean). The sparks flew and a DFMO ensued. Later, they had an official first date at the First Friday art festival in Philly

  • December 29, 2012

    Making it Official

    Winter break, Tom went with his family to Hawaii and Youn traveled with Emma Gow, Best Woman to Costa Rica. Skype calls did not cut it, so Tom traded one tropical paradise for another and booked a last minute flight to Costa Rica. That pretty much sealed the deal

  • April 20, 2013

    Meeting the Parents

    By the time Tom graduated from Wharton, things were getting pretty serious. Tom and Youn traveled to Seoul to meet Youn’s family. He returned again in summer of 2014 to formally ask for Youn’s hand, and both families got together in Seoul for New Year’s 2015

  • August 15, 2014

    Move to SF

    After Youn finished Wharton, she and Tom moved from the East Coast to their current place in San Francisco

  • October 18, 2014

    Proposal

    Youn and Tom took a weekend wine tasting trip to Sonoma. Youn was suspicious because Tom shaved on Saturday, but she had some doubts because he wore shorts. Toward the end of a beautiful day exploring vineyards, Tom bent his exposed knee, and she said yes!
